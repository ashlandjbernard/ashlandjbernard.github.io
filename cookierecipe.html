Chocolate Chip OREO Cookie Bars ©

«by Ashland Bernard»

a little about this ooey gooey bar

 I have quite the treat for you today! Chocolate chip OREO cookie bars. Whether you need a fixing for some ooey gooey goodness, have the ingredients laying down, have a new neighbor in the 'hood, or are just plain bored: try this recipe on for size! I promise you it will not disappoint!
If you need anymore inspiration or a little nudge in the direction of cooking these delicious little bars...as our dear friend Julia Childs says, “Find something you're passionate about and keep tremendously interested in it.” For us here at Cooking Cookies Central, cooking is it!









Preparation
  1. Prep time: 30 min
  2. Cook time: 30 min
  3. Total time: 1 hour
  4. Yield: 2 dozen cookies

Ingredients
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • ½ cup salted butter (softened)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 ½ cup flour
  • 2 ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 12 OREO cookies (crushed)
  • ½ cup white chocolate chips
  • ½ cup milk chocolate chips
    ***Optional: Top with 2 extra OREO cookies and a ¼ cup white chocolate chips

    Instructions
    1. Pre-heat oven to 325 degrees.
    2. Cream butter and sugar in a large bowl with a mixer.
    3. Add in eggs and vanilla and stir until combined.
    4. Mix the baking powder and salt with 1 cup of flour.
    5. Pour into the butter mixture and stir in the rest of the flour.
    6. Fold in OREO's and chocolate chips.
    7. Line a 13x9 with foil and press dough into pan with flour on your hands.
    8. Place in oven and bake for 25-35 minutes on until lightly browned. Oven temps will vary. Test with a tooth pick.
    9. If you would like you may take the bars out 15 minutes in and top with OREO's and white chocolate chips.
    10. Allow bars to cool and enjoy with a cold glass of milk!
